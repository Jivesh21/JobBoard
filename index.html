Support

Need help? Here's everything you need to know.

💾 Where is my data stored?
All your job data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to a server, so your data stays completely private to your device.
🔔 How do interview alerts work?
Set an Interview Date and Time when adding or editing a job. You'll see a banner and browser notification on the day of and the day before the interview.
📱 Can I install this as an app?
Yes! Go to Settings and click "Install App", or look for the install icon in your browser's address bar.
🖱️ How do I move a job between stages?
Simply drag and drop the card from one column to another on the Dashboard's Board view. Works on both desktop and mobile.
